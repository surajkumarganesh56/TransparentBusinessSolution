Vision, Mission, Objectives

Vision Statement

To be the leading provider of innovative outsourcing services, recognized for our commitment to excellence, client success, and transformative impact on businesses worldwide.

Mission Statement

To empower businesses by delivering comprehensive outsourcing solutions—including counseling, scheme and asset monitoring, stock verification, damage destruction, import facilitation, channel management support, executive recruitment, export buyer leads, and employee outsourcing—that drive efficiency, foster growth, and create lasting value.

Objectives

1. Client-Centric Excellence: o Deliver high-quality, customized outsourcing solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. o Achieve a client satisfaction rate of over 90% within the first year of operations. 2. Operational Efficiency: o Implement streamlined processes to ensure timely and cost-effective service delivery. o Reduce operational costs by 15% within the first two years through continuous improvement initiatives. 3. Market Expansion: o Establish a strong presence in the local market within the first year. o Expand services to at least two international markets within the first three years. 4. Innovation and Growth: o Invest in technology and training to stay ahead of industry trends. o Develop and launch at least two new service offerings within the first two years. 5. Employee Development: o Provide ongoing training and professional development opportunities for all staff. o Achieve an employee retention rate of 85% or higher within the first year. These statements and objectives are designed to align your company's strategic direction with its core services and values, ensuring a clear path toward achieving your business goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our Auditing services in Nepal

What services does your auditing firm provide?

We specialize in statutory audits, internal audits, tax audits, and compliance audits. Additionally, we provide financial advisory and due diligence services tailored to Nepal's regulations.

How do you ensure compliance with Nepal's auditing standards?

Our team adheres to the Nepal Standards on Auditing (NSA) and International Standards on Auditing (ISA), ensuring thorough and compliant auditing processes for every client.

Do you provide advisory services alongside audits?

Yes, our services include financial advisory, risk assessment, and guidance on improving internal controls to help businesses achieve greater efficiency and transparency.

What is your approach to auditing financial statements?

We follow a systematic approach by reviewing financial records, evaluating internal controls, and ensuring compliance with applicable standards. Our process culminates in a detailed and transparent audit report.

Can you assist with audit preparation and documentation?

Yes, we help clients prepare for audits by organizing financial documents, identifying potential issues, and ensuring readiness for compliance checks.

What sectors do you cater to?

We serve diverse sectors including manufacturing, trading, NGOs, financial institutions, and government organizations, with tailored auditing solutions for each.
